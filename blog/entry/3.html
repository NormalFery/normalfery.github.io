So you may think "What's the problem?" Well... In Splatoon we have these things called "Mirror matches", where if after a certain period of matchmaking time, there aren't enough enemy team members to battle against, you'll just...

Battle your own team, which won't count toward the results!...

You can see the problem now, right?

Here's the thing tho, mirror matches have been a thing for years, and while when there were two teams, it wouldn't matter, since it'd be a 60/40%, but when there's 3 teams, now if it's more than a 40%, you're 100% guaranteed to loose by mirror matches.

So here's what I think should be done about it :

First, remove mirror matches. I know it may mean having the "Not enough players" error, but at least matches would count.

Secondly, balance the options??? Don't just give a single team the best option, since it'd mean many people flock to it.
