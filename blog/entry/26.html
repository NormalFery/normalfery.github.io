Deltarune Chapters 3 and 4

So as you may know, last week, Chapters 3 & 4 of Deltarune released and... OH MY GOD.

First off, I replayed Chapters 1 and 2 from scratch, just to have that save data for a normal playthrough (Most if not all recruits, and not much more)

So starting Chapter 3 in the dark world, and... wow. Ralsei and Susie having a talk, just for FUCKING TENNA TO APPEAR AND DRAG US TO A SHOW

Of course, in name input, I went with "KRS", just because I could. I didn't even realize it could be read as "KuRSe" and got confused as the joke, then realized.

The gameplay was quite nice too, and it was really hilarious in every way. After the second board though, and the story picking up, I kinda... really hated the whole Tenna taking control part, and just being forced to battle and such, but after figuring out the shut down button thing, I felt so smart

The gameplay, everything, felt amazing! Even the water cooler. The boss fight? Amazing? And the Knight? One shot me :3 And I have no shadow mantle.

The whole shelter thing and everything, and the ending being THAT, just was like "OH MY GOD WHAT".

As for Chapter 4, seeing Gerson I was like "excuse me what", because I follow the lore and seeing him there was something. I also heard Undyne's theme in his (or would it be the opposite?) and just imploded. Like the recognition was IMMEDIATE.

THe lore, the battles, THE MUSIC, god I loved it all SO MUCH I just loved the both chapters.

Also hearing Undertale Music in Deltarune made me go "OMG I KNOW THAT FROM UNDERTALE", lmfao

As for the non dark world lore, ALSO AMAZING, and CAROL??????.

Lastly, just to finish this before I just go insane, I just wanna say... i can't wait for the next chapters :sob:
