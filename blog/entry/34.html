Cultural Pass!

So, since I'll be turning 18 soon (look at the next section to know when :3), I was able to (using my mom to represent me), request the "Cultural Pass"!

What's a cultural pass? It's just a debit card the government gives people who are turning 18 this year for culture stuff! In specific, we get:

  • 200€ for "Cultural Events" (Movies, Theater, Etc..)
  • 100€ for Digital Purchases (Gift cards, mostly)
  • 100€ for Physical Goods (Books, CDs/DVDs, etc...)

But of course, it comes with a caveat at the very least. Which is the fact that the only stores available are the ones that request it via paperwork, and the government accepts. So for example, a cinema closer to my home isn't available, so I have to take two buses to the 2nd nearest, which is further away.

There's also the fact that 50% of the money is stuck in a category that most people never use, except for rare occasions, so that sucks, but oh well

The good thing is... I got Deltarune for free :3c (LEGALLY)
