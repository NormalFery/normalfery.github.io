Unconvalidated Convalidations

So, as you may know, I'm doing "Superior Vocational School" currently, to expand my knowledge, and not be 17 and do nothing. Of course, that does bring one new thing I can do! Good old convalidations.

For those who may not know, or if my mindless translation is wrong, a convalidation is a way to "pass" a subject without doing it, by already having done it before, or having the same knowledge as it (Without an exam).
For example, my certified B2 in English allows me to convalidate my English class, so once it's processed, I just don't have that subject anymore!

If only it was that simple though.

I give my paperwork to the head teacher of my class, so he can forward it to the Secretary's Office, so I can convalidate English and "ITK", sounds good, right? I thought so too for ~a month, since they don't process anything until the time ends for the presentation.
